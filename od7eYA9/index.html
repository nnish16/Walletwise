WalletWise

Spend Smarter, Not Harder.

WalletWise is the intelligent co-pilot for your spending, empowering you to effortlessly maximize credit card rewards with every single transaction.

The Problem

The Credit Card Shell Game

Professionals juggle multiple credit cards in a complex, opaque rewards landscape. The cognitive load of tracking rotating categories, bonus multipliers, and sign-up offers is immense. This leads to the fear of missing out (FOMO) on hundreds or thousands of dollars in rewards annually, simply because the right choice is too difficult to determine in the moment.

The Solution

Clarity is a Feature.

WalletWise is your intelligent financial co-pilot. We productize and personalize financial guidance, transforming the complexity of credit card rewards into a simple, actionable recommendation. No more guesswork, no more spreadsheetsâ€”just the right card, for the right purchase, every time.

Your Personal Finance Power-Up

WalletWise delivers immediate value with a clever, rules-based engine focused on what matters most.

Location-Based Recommendations

About to pay? WalletWise knows where you are and instantly tells you which of your cards offers the best rewards for that specific store or spending category, ensuring you maximize every purchase.

Never Miss a Bonus

Visually track your progress towards hitting lucrative sign-up bonuses. Our dashboard shows how much you've spent and how many days you have left, so you never leave a welcome offer on the table again.

Your Entire Wallet, Simplified

Manage all your cards from a single, beautifully designed dashboard. Manually track balances and due dates to stay organized and avoid late fees, without juggling multiple banking apps.

Product Vision

The Future is an Automated Financial Co-Pilot

V1: The Rules Engine (MVP)

Validate the core loop with manual card entry, a rules-based recommendation engine, and a beautifully designed SUB tracker.

V2: The Automation Engine

Reduce manual input via Plaid integration for automated transactions and introduce AI-driven recommendations for new cards.

V3+: The Financial Co-Pilot

Become an indispensable tool with credit score tracking, bill pay, and proactive alerts for financial opportunities.
